Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

WightMare ("we", "our", or "us") operates the WightMare game available on the web at wightmare.com and on Android via Google Play. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information needed to operate the game:

  • Gamertag – a display name you choose freely. This is stored locally on your device and submitted to our leaderboard when you finish a game.
  • Game scores – your high scores, stored alongside your gamertag on our leaderboard (powered by Google Firebase Firestore).

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, or any other personal information. We do not require account registration.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display your score on the in-game leaderboard.
  • To persist your gamertag locally so you don't have to re-enter it.

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties beyond the Firebase services used to store leaderboard data.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase (Firestore) to store leaderboard entries. Firebase processes data in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

Data Retention

Leaderboard entries (gamertag + score) are retained indefinitely to maintain the leaderboard. You may request deletion of your entry by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

WightMare is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@wightmare.com.
